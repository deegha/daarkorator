Pro Tips

  • Take time to look through the design brief to understand what the customer is looking for in the design.
  • Pay attention to the budget.
  • Leverage style boards you might already have as a starting point where it makes sense.
  • Only use online vendors for the products you include in your style boards. Everything you recommend must be available online for purchase.
  • Your initial style board should give our customer a good feel for the design concept you are proposing. If your style board is picked as the winner, you will get a chance later to tweak and finalize the style board to the customer's liking.
  • Consider including paint colours, curtains, accessories and lighting in addition to furniture in your style boards.
